Subcription Plans

Netflix brings entertainment for everyone with flexible subscription plans. Choose the plan that fits your lifestyle and budget. Enjoy unlimited movies and TV shows with no ads, and stream on any device, anytime.Share the joy with family and friends. With multiple profiles and personalized recommendations, everyone gets their own Netflix experience. Plus, download your favorites to watch offline, wherever you are.


Premium

Rs 1,100/month
Enjoy ultra HD streaming, downloads on multiple devices, and simultaneous viewing for the whole family with the Netflix Premium Plan.



  • Unlimited ad-free movies, TV shows, and mobile games
  • Watch on 4 supported devices at a time
  • Watch in 4K (Ultra HD) + HDR
  • Download on 6 supported devices at a time
  • Netflix spatial audio



Standard

Rs 800/month
Stream your favorite shows in full HD and enjoy viewing on two devices at the same time with the Netflix Standard Plan.





  • Unlimited ad-free movies, TV shows, and mobile games
  • Watch on 2 supported devices at a time
  • Watch in 1080p (Full HD)
  • Download on 2 supported devices at a time




Basic

Rs 450/month
Enjoy unlimited movies and TV shows in standard quality on a single device with the affordable Netflix Basic Plan.





  • Unlimited ad-free movies, TV shows, and mobile games
  • Watch on 1 supported devices at a time
  • Watch in 720p (HD)
  • Download on 1 supported devices at a time
